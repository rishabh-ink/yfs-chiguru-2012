Youth for Seva - Chiguru ’ 12

Day of Joy!

For the fourth consecutive year, Youth for Seva is organizing Chiguru, an inter-school competition for the government school children in the cultural arena.

Chiguru provides a platform for those not-so-priviledged children, who are from the government schools, to give wings to their cultural prowess — be it dancing, singing, drawing, pick-and-speak etc. It is a day dedicated to fulfilling the hidden dreams of these children through an inter-school competition.

This year, we are expecting more than 1,500 children from 40 government schools, who will be participating in 17 competitions.
